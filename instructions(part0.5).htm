PAYMENT

You will be paid 2 USD for answering the quiz questions correctly.

You will be paid an additional 10 USD for completing the experiment.

Droping Out from the Experiment

If you drop out before the game play, you will not receive any payment.

If you drop out at any time during the game play, you and everyone playing in your session will only receive 2 USD.

Payment from the Game Play

You can make at best an additional 12 USD (a final net payment of 24 USD).

You can at worst lose 7 USD (a final net payment of 5 USD).

On average, you should expect to make an additional 3 USD (a final net payment of 15 USD).

Currency

During the game, you will use tokens as the experimental currency.

1 token is worth 0.6 cents.
