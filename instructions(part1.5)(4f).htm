Decision Screen for Leader

Since both leaders make an investment decision at the same time, you need to consider an hypothetical value for the opponent's decision and make your investment decision accordingly.

As on the follower's decision screen, to facilitate your decision process, we provide you with the below graphical tool. You can consider your decision either on section or on section. Changing the value in one section will automatically change the value on the other. If you do not click the Done button before the decision period ends, your last entry on either of the decision sections will be considered as your final decision.

Since leaders' decisions are made after the followers' help or sabotage decision. Leaders will also have a graphical display as in where all followers' decisions are shown. Again note that based on these decision, relative investment power of leaders are determined.
