Relative Investment Power

The effectiveness of tokens invested on the wheel of fortune may differ between the competitors. As a result, players may differ in their investment power .

As a player's investment power increases, she can claim a larger share of the wheel for every token she invests on the wheel.

The effectiveness of each player's tokens are determined before the investment decisions stage begins and declared to all involved parties at the beginning of the stage.

Leaders do not have the ability to increase the effectiveness of their tokens and can only make investment decisions based on their predetermined investment powers.

Use by scrolling ◄ ► to consider different relative investment power levels for you and your opponent.

You can increase your relative power by sliding ◄ ► to right.

Values inside ◄ ► represent the relative investment power of each player's tokens.

For instance, 5 ◄ ► 1 means that every token you invested is worth 5 tokens invested by your opponent.
