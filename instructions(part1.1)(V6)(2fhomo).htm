In the experiment, you will encounter 2 different types of contests:
Leaders' Contest and Followers' Contest.

Leaders' Contest

Leaders compete in the leaders' contest.

Winner

- Receives 1000 tokens

- Proceeds to the next leaders' contest

- Her followers continue as followers.

Loser

- Loses her leader role.

- Becomes a follower.

- Her followers determine the new leader
in the followers' contest.

In the leaders' contest, regardless of the results, followers always receive 0 token.

Click for followers' contest

Followers' Contest

Lost leader's followers compete in the followers' contest

Winner

- Becomes the new leader of the group

- Proceeds to the next leaders' contest.

Loser

- Continues as a follower.

Followers' contest is played only among the 2 followers of the lost leader.

The lost leader does not participate in the followers' contest.
