EXPERIMENT

The experiment consists of 10 periods

Every period, you will play a game that consists of 6 stages

You will start every period with 550 tokens

At the beginning of the experiment, you will be assigned to the role of leader or follower.

You will start every period with this initially assigned role.

During a single period, a change in your role will not carryover to the next period.
