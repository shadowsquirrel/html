Help and Sabotage

Before a leaders' contest, followers decide to help or sabotage their own leader.

These decisions determine the power of their leaders.

Through help or sabotage, followers can make their leader advantageous or disadvantageous in the leaders' contest.

Help increases a leader's purchasing power

Sabotage decreases a leader's purchasing power

It is costly to help or sabotage.

One unit of help / sabotage costs 1 token.

When followers do not sabotage or help their own leader, Leaders have equal investment power.

Small amounts of help or sabotage by Follower A has a significant impact when Follower B is not helping or sabotaging.

Impact of help or sabotage does not depend on the absolute levels of leaders' investments.

Impact of help or sabotage depends on the ratio of leaders' investments.

The impact of Follower A's sabotage on his leader A depends on Follower B's sabotage on his leader B.

The impact of Follower A's help on his leader A depends on Follower B's help on his leader B.
