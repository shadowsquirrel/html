Decision Screen for Followers

Since every follower from both groups will make an help or sabotage decision at the same time and before leaders make their investment decisions, you will need to consider hypothetical investment decisions for all the other players (other followers and leaders) and make your investment decision accordingly.

To facilitate your decision process, we provide you with the below graphical tool. You can consider your decision either on section or on section. Changing the value in one section will automatically change the value on the other. section is provided to enable you to make a more precise decision.

During the experiment, you will have a limited amount of time to make your decision. If you make your decision before the time limit is reached, you can click the Done button on the top right corner to submit your decision. If you do not click the Done button before the decision period ends, your last entry on either of the decision sections will be considered as your final decision.
