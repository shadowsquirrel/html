Below is your main decision section.

You will have a limited time to make your decision by chosing a help/sabotage value inside .

When you are done, click the Done button on the top right corner to submit your decision.

If you fail to submit within the time limit, your current entry will be considered as your final decision.

Move the slider left to sabotage, right to help.

Under the decision slider, net payoffs for possible contest results are displayed.

The help/sabotage decision requires you to consider what the other players will possibly do.

We provide you a graphical calculator to facilitate this decision process.

Note:
Sliders will be disabled.
At the end, you will have time to get accustomed to the calculator on your own.

Leaders' hypothetical investment decisions and each leader's probability to win are displayed on the below section.

You can explore what leaders will do given any hypothetical purchasing powers under various help/sabotage scenarios.

Please experiment with the calculator.
