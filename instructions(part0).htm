Welcome

Welcom to our experiment!

In this experiment, you will be playing a game that consists of 6 stages where you will be randomly assigned to a group and to the role leader or follower.

You will be playing the same game 10 times and every time you will be re-assigned to a new group and a new role.

The entire experiment will last about 75 minutes.

First, you will be introduced to the stages of the game, then you will be introduced to the mechanics of the game.

Once you are done with these introduction, you will have a short quiz testing your understanding of the game. If you fail to answer any of the questions correctly, you will not be qualified to play the experiment.

After the quiz, you will practice the game against computer.

Once you are done with these stages, you will begin the experiment. The experiment will last for 50 minutes.

You will start every period of the game with 1000 tokens. 1 token is worth half a penny.

After every period, your net payoff will be calculated.

At the end of the experiment, you will be paid x USD for your participation.

In addition, one of your net payoffs from the 10 periods you have played will be randomly selected and be paid.

You have the possibility to make a maximum of 2000 tokens from any period of the game depending on your role and the other players you will be playing against.
