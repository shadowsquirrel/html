Welcome to our experiment!

The experiment consists of 15 minutes of instuctions followed by 60 minutes of repeated game play.

You will be playing a game multiple times.

Every time, you will be randomly assigned to a group, an opposing group, and to a role.

Every time, you start as the leader or as a follower of your group.

Leaders of two opposing groups compete in a leadership contest.

Followers of each group can help or sabotage their leader

They may also compete in a followers' contest.

You will first be introduced to the various dynamics of a contest.

You will then be introduced to the structure of the game.

After this instruction section, you will take a short quiz and proceed to playing the game.
