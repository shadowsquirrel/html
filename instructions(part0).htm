Welcome to our experiment!

The experiment consists of 15 minutes of instuctions followed by 60 minutes of repeated game play.

At the beginning of the game, you will be assigned to the role of leader or follower.

At every repetition of the game, you will be assigned to a random group consisting of 2(4) followers and 1 leader and randomly match against a group with the same composition.

The basic structure of the game

First, followers decide whether to help or sabotage their own leader

Then, leaders compete with each other in a leadership contest .

Details will be given in the instruction section.
