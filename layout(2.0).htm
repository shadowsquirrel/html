STAGE 1

Assignement of Roles

You will be randomly assigned:

-To the role of leader of follower

-To a group of 2(4) followers and 1 leader

-To an opposing group with the same size and composition as yours

STAGE 2

Followers' Sabotage or Help Decision I




Each follower of each group decides on the level of sabotage or help to his leader

STAGE 3

Leaders' Contest I




Leaders are informed on the decision of both groups' followers.

Leaders compete in a leaders' contest by investing tokens.

Each token will worth more or less depending on the level of help or sabotage received by her followers.

If the leader wins in Stage 3

Stage 4 does NOT occur




Each Follower:

-Loses the amount of tokens invested in sabotage or help.

-Continues the game as a follower and proceeds to Stage 5.

Leader

-Receives 1000 tokens and loses the amount of tokens invested in the Leaders' Contest I

-Continues as the leader of the group and proceeds to Stage 6.

If the leader loses in Stage 3

Stage 4 occurs.




Each Follower:

-Loses the amount of tokens invested in sabotage or help.

Gets the chance to become the new leader.

Leader:

-Loses the amount of tokens invested in the Leaders' Contest I

-Loses her role as the leader, becomes a follower and proceeds to Stage 5.

STAGE 4

Followers' Contest




Occurs only for the followers of the leader who lost in Stage 3

Followers compete in a contest by investing tokens for the new leadership position of their group


If a follower loses

-Loses the amount of tokens invested in the contest.

Continues as a follower and proceeds to Stage 5.




if a follower wins

-Loses the amount of tokens invested in the contest

-Becomes the new leader of her group and proceeds to Stage 6.
