Purchasing Power

Players may have different purchasing powers.

A player with relatively high purchasing power can get a larger share of the wheel for every token spent.

Purchasing powers are determined always before a contest and do not change during the contest.

Relative Purchasing Power

Values inside ◄ ► represent the per token relative purchasing power of players.

For instance, 5 ◄ ► 1 implies that every token you invest is worth 5 tokens invested by the opponent.
