Game Basics

Leaders of each group compete with each other by investing tokens on a wheel of fortune. The more a leader invests tokens relative to her opponent, the bigger share of the wheel of fortune she occupies.

Use sections to consider investment levels for you and the opponent.

After both leaders make their investment decisions, the wheel is turned. When it stops, the owner of the region that ▼ points to is declared as the winner.

Both leaders lose the tokens they have invested and the winner receives 1000 tokens.

1 token is worth half a penny.
