Relative Investment Power

Players may have different investment power.

A player with relatively high investment power can have a larger share of the wheel for every token spent.

Investment power does not depend on the investment level.

Relative Investment Power

Values inside ◄ ► represent the per unit relative investment power of players.

For instance, 5 ◄ ► 1 implies that every token you invest is worth 5 tokens invested by the opponent.

Click for examples.

A player with high power can occupy a significant portion of the wheel with relatively low amount of tokens invested.

A player with low power has a disadvantage in the contest.

Followers' Contest

Lost leader (of the leaders' contest) loses her leader role and becomes a follower.

Lost leader's followers proceed to the followers' contest

Winner

- Becomes the new leader of the group

- Proceeds to the next leaders' contest to compete against the winner of the former leaders' contest.

Loser

- Continues as a follower.

Followers' contest is played only among the followers of the lost leader.

The lost leader does not participate in the followers' contest.
