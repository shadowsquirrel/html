Help and Sabotage

Each leader's relatve effectiveness of their tokens is determined by her followers.

Prior to leaders' competition, followers of each leader affect the effectiveness of her leader's relative investment power by helping or sabotaging her leader.

Each follower separately decides on how many tokens to invest in either helping or sabotaging her leader.

A follower can increase her leader's relative investment power by investing tokens in help or decrease it by investing tokens in sabotage.

As in the leaders' case, 1 token is worth half a penny.

After followers make their sabotage or help decision, relative investment powers of leaders are determined and leaders proceed to compete with each other.

Regardless of whether a leader loses or wins the competition, her followers receive 0 token.

But, when a leader loses, she also loses her leadership position, and her followers get the chance to become the new leader of their group and to compete for 1000 tokens If there is a new leaders competition.

use sections to consider help or sabotage levels for both leaders.

Move the slider up to select an help level.

Move the slider down to select a sabotage level.

Notice how the relative investment powers change based on the relative help and sabotage levels received by each leader.
